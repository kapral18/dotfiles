What's the single smartest and most radically innovative and accretive and
useful and compelling addition you could make at this point to the local
changes?

Only suggest if you think the change is smart, innovative, accretive, useful,
and compelling. It's ok to refrain from doing so if you don't have a good
suggestion, because no change is sometimes better than a marginally better
change.
